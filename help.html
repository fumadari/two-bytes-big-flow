Broadcast Gain: What’s Going On

Goal: show how a tiny, neighbor‑only 2‑byte broadcast trims long‑tail wait and boosts flow under lossy comms, without retraining.

BG OFF: fixed min‑green, naive switch on queue diff → more idle‑green and knife‑edge stalls.

BG ON: near‑gate cars send 2 bytes (urgency + axis/distance). Neighbors fuse into a light consensus + confidence cue to stretch greens under weak info and tilt switch decisions under strong info. A tiny nudge helps the lead car on green break ties (never on red).
